Text:    The Query retrieves the shipping priority and potential revenue, defined as the sum of extended price * (1-discount), of the orders having the largest revenue among those that had not been shipped as of '1998-09-16'. Orders are listed in decreasing order of revenue. If more than 10 unshipped orders exist, only the 10 orders with the largest revenue are listed.
SELECT o.O_SHIPPRIORITY, SUM(wl.WL_EXTENDEDPRICE * (1 - wl.WL_DISCOUNT)) AS potential_revenue
FROM orders o
JOIN web_lineitem wl ON o.O_ORDERKEY = wl.WL_ORDERKEY
WHERE o.O_ORDERDATE <= '1998-09-16' AND wl.WL_SHIPDATE > '1998-09-16'
GROUP BY o.O_SHIPPRIORITY, o.O_ORDERKEY
ORDER BY potential_revenue DESC
LIMIT 10;
